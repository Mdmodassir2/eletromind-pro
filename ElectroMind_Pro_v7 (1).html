⚡ Bijli Kya Hoti Hai?
⚡ Bilkul simple bhasha mein — koi bhi samajh sakta hai!
💡
Bijli = Electrons ka flow
Jaise paani pipe mein behta hai — waise hi bijli wire mein behti hai. Is behne ko "Current" kehte hain. Jitna zyada paani bahega, utni zyada bijli.
🔋
Voltage Kya Hai?
Voltage = Pressure. Jaise paani tank mein upar se pressure aata hai — waise hi bijli mein voltage pressure deta hai. India mein ghar mein 230 Volt hoti hai.
💧
Current Kya Hai?
Current = Kitni bijli beh rahi hai. Ampere (A) mein measure hota hai. Jitna bada appliance, utna zyada current lega. Bulb = 0.4A, AC = 8-10A.
⚡
AC aur DC Kya Hai?
AC (Alternating Current) = Ghar mein jo bijli aati hai — aage peeche chalti hai. DC (Direct Current) = Battery mein — ek direction mein chalti hai. Mobile charger AC ko DC mein convert karta hai.
🏠
Ghar Mein Bijli Kaise Aati Hai?
Power station → Transformer → Aapke meter → DB panel → Switches → Appliances. Bijli ka safar poora ho jata hai jab current appliance se wapas neutral wire se return karta hai.
💡 Yaad rakho: Bijli se pyaar karo — lekin darr ke saath! Hamesha safe rehna.
🔌 Wire Kya Hoti Hai?
🔴
Live Wire (Phase Wire)
Ye wire bijli lekar aati hai — sabse dangerous. India mein Red color ki hoti hai. Gulf mein Brown color. KABHI BHI haath mat lagao bina test kiye!
⚫
Neutral Wire
Ye wire bijli wapas lekar jati hai. India mein Black ya Blue color. Ye bhi dangerous ho sakti hai — hamesha test karo.
🟢
Earth Wire (Safety Wire)
Ye wire safety ke liye hai — agar koi fault ho toh bijli seedha zameen mein chali jati hai aur aapko shock nahi lagta. Green-Yellow color. KABHI mat hatao!
📏
Wire Size Kya Hoti Hai?
Wire ki thickness mm² mein hoti hai. 1.5mm² = Lights ke liye (patli). 2.5mm² = Socket ke liye (medium). 4mm² = AC/Geyser ke liye (moti). Jitna bada load, utni moti wire chahiye.
⚠️ Kabhi bhi naked (insulation hataya hua) wire ko haath mat lagao — jaan ja sakti hai!
🔘 Switch Kya Hota Hai?
🔘
Switch Ka Kaam
Switch ek "gate" hai jo Live wire ko kholta aur band karta hai. ON karo — bijli chalti hai. OFF karo — bijli band. Switch hamesha Live wire pe lagta hai — Neutral pe nahi.
1️⃣
1-Way Switch
Ek jagah se on/off hota hai. Ghar mein sabse common. Ek switch — ek light ya fan.
2️⃣
2-Way Switch
Do jagah se on/off hota hai. Seedhi aur neechli manzil pe — dono jagah se light control karo. Seedhiyon (stairs) ke upar-neeche aam use hota hai.
🔌
Socket (Plug Point)
Switch nahi hai — hamesha ON rehta hai. Isliye alag switch lagta hai socket ke saath. 5A socket = chhote loads. 15A socket = bade loads jaise AC, geyser.
💡 Switch tod ke andar wires mat chhuoo — pehle MCB band karo!
⚡ MCB Kya Hota Hai?
🛡️
MCB = Miniature Circuit Breaker
Woh chota sa switch jo automatically trip (band) ho jata hai jab bijli zyada ho jaye ya short circuit ho. Aapki safety ka guardian hai!
🔥
MCB Trip Kyun Hota Hai?
1) Overload — bahut saare appliances ek saath chala liye. 2) Short circuit — koi wire galat jagah touch kar gayi. 3) Faulty appliance — koi ek cheez kharab ho gayi.
🔄
MCB Reset Kaise Karein?
Step 1: Sab appliances band karo. Step 2: MCB lever pura neeche dabao. Step 3: Phir upar karo. Step 4: Ek ek karke appliance on karo — jis pe trip ho woh faulty hai.
❓
MCB vs Purana Fuse
Purana fuse = ek baar jalega toh replace karna padega. MCB = reset ho jata hai. MCB better, safer aur convenient hai. Apne ghar mein MCB lagwao!
✅ MCB trip hona normal hai — ye kaam kar raha hai! Ghabrao mat.
🔌 Plug Kaise Lagayein?
⚠️ Pehle socket ka switch OFF karo. Phir plug lagao.
🔌
Plug Lagane Ka Sahi Tarika
1) Socket switch OFF karo. 2) Plug seedha daalo — force mat lagao. 3) Phir switch ON karo. Kabhi bhi live socket mein haath mat daalo!
⚠️
Dangerous Galtiyan Jo Mat Karo
❌ Ek socket mein bahut saare adapter mat lagao. ❌ Damaged plug mat use karo. ❌ Geele haathon se plug mat lagao. ❌ Wire se khichke plug mat nikalo.
✅
Sahi Tarike Se Plug Nikalna
Switch OFF karo pehle. Phir plug ke body (plastic part) ko pakad ke nikalo — wire ko kheench ke mat nikalo. Wire damage hoti hai.
🔥
Extension Board Ka Dhyan
Extension board ek hi circuit pe bahut saara load dal deta hai. Overloading se aag lag sakti hai. Maximum load check karo — zyada mat lagao.
⚠️ Safe Kaise Rahein?
🚨 Bijli se har saal hazaron log marte hain — sirf thodi si savdhani se ye roka ja sakta hai!
🖐️
Kabhi Haath Mat Lagao
Kisi bhi wire ya socket mein bina switch off kiye haath mat lagao. Ye sabse basic aur important rule hai.
💧
Paani Aur Bijli — Dushman Hain
Bathroom mein, kitchen mein, baarish mein — extra careful raho. Geele haath + bijli = bahut dangerous combination.
🔥
Overloading Se Bachao
Ek socket mein zyada appliances mat lagao. Agar wire garam ho rahi hai — ye overloading ka sign hai. Fire ho sakti hai!
👀
In Signs Ko Seriously Lo
🔴 Bijli ka bill achanak badh gaya. 🔴 Socket ya switch garm lag raha hai. 🔴 Burning smell aa rahi hai. 🔴 Lights dim hoti hain. In sab mein electrician bulao!
🚨
Emergency Mein
Koi shock lage → PEHLE main switch band karo → Phir help karo. Paani mat dalo! Fire pe CO₂ extinguisher use karo. Emergency: 112.
👷 Electrician Kab Bulayein?
✅ Kuch kaam aap khud kar sakte ho — kuch ke liye expert zaroor bulao!
✅ YE KHUD KAR SAKTE HO
✅
Bulb / Tube Change Karna
Switch off karo → bulb nikalo → naya lagao → test karo. Simple!
✅
MCB Reset Karna
Sab appliances band karo → lever neeche phir upar. Done!
✅
Plug Repair (Simple)
Appliance unplug karo → plug kholo → tight karo → band karo.
🚫 EXPERT BULAO — KHUD MAT KARO
🚫
Naya Wiring Karna
Poore ghar ki wiring — ya kisi bhi room mein naya wiring — expert ka kaam hai. Galat wiring se fire ho sakti hai.
🚫
DB Panel Ka Kaam
MCB change karna, new circuit add karna — electrician ka kaam. Galat connection se serious accident ho sakta hai.
🚫
Meter Se Related Kuch Bhi
Energy meter ke aas paas kuch bhi mat karo — ye bijli board ki property hai. Tamper karna illegal bhi hai.
🚫
Koi Bhi Burning Smell Ya Spark
Agar kuch jal raha lage ya spark dikh raha ho — expert bulao TURANT. Ye serious problem hai.
🔧 Basic Tools
🔧 Ye tools har ghar mein hone chahiye — emergency mein kaam aate hain.
🔩
Screwdriver Set
Flat aur pilla (Phillips) dono. Switch board, plug repair ke liye. Insulated handle hona zaroor — current nahi lagega.
🔦
Test Lamp / Neon Tester
Wire live hai ya nahi — check karne ke liye. ₹30-50 mein milta hai. Kisi bhi kaam se pehle test karo. MUST HAVE!
🔦
Torch / Flashlight
Bijli jate hi andhera — torch kaam aayegi. Phone torch bhi chalega. DB panel dekhnk ke liye zaroor chahiye.
🔨
Pliers
Wire pakadne ke liye. Insulated handle wala lena — metal handle dangerous hai.
🩹
Electrical Tape
Bare wire ko cover karne ke liye. Black ya red color. Temporary fix ke liye — permanent solution ke liye electrician bulao.
💡 Ye tools ₹200-300 mein aa jate hain — ghar mein zaroor rakho!

Clamp Meter

Wire Stripper

Combination Pliers

Nose Pliers (Long Nose)

Hammer Drill Machine

MDB — Main Distribution Board
+
Building ka sabse pehla LT panel. Main incomer MCCB (400A–1600A). Copper ya aluminium busbar. Sab SMDB/DB ko power yahan se milti hai.
IMDB — Intermediate Main DB
+
Bade buildings mein MDB aur SMDB ke beech. Floor-wise ya zone-wise power distribution ke liye.
SMDB — Sub Main DB
+
Floor ya zone level pe. Typical rating: 100A–400A. Riser cables yahan se final DB tak.
DB — Distribution Board (Final)
+
End user ke paas. Lights, sockets, AC ke liye MCB/RCCB. Domestic: 4–16 way. IS:8623 standard.
PDB — Power Distribution Board
+
Sirf power loads (motors, heavy equipment) ke liye dedicated panel.

Site Pe Shortcuts
📏
Cable Length Estimate
Apna qadam measure karo (typically 2.5 feet). Walk karke roughly length calculate karo. Conduit ke liye 10-15% extra add karo.
🧲
Conduit Mein Wire Dalne Ka Trick
Pehle fish tape ya nylon rope daal do. Cable tape karo rope ke saath. Talcum powder ya cable gel use karo friction kam karne ke liye.
📱
Phone Ka Smart Use
Kisi bhi connection ka photo khincho pehle — baad mein reference ke liye. Panel wiring, junction box — sab photo rakho.
🎯
MCB Labelling
Har MCB pe label lagao — "Lights Floor 1", "AC Bedroom". Baad mein aur doosre electricians ka kaam asaan hoga.
🔦
Kaam Shuru Karne Se Pehle
1) Power off. 2) Test lamp se verify. 3) Lock/Tag lagao. 4) Phir haath lagao. 30 second ki safety jaan bachati hai.
Wire Identification Tips
🎨
Color Se Pehchano
India: Red/Yellow/Blue = Phase. Gulf/IEC: Brown/Black/Grey = Phase. Site pe pehle standard confirm karo.
⚡
Test Lamp Se Identify
Live wire: Lamp jalega. Neutral/Earth: Nahi jalega. Hamesha test karo — color pe trust mat karo akele.
📐
Wire Size Estimate
1.5mm² ≈ 1.4mm dia. 2.5mm² ≈ 1.8mm. 4mm² ≈ 2.3mm. 6mm² ≈ 2.8mm.
🔢
Cable Markings Padho
Har cable pe print: size (mm²), voltage rating, standard. Jaise "2.5mm² 1100V IS:694".
Fault Finding Methods
🔍
Half Split Method
Circuit ko beech se check karo. Agar theek hai toh problem doosri taraf. Phir us half ko phir beech se check karo.
👁️
Visual Inspection Pehle
Broken wires, burnt marks, loose terminals — 70% faults visual inspection se mil jate hain.
👃
Smell Se Pata Karo
Burning smell = overheating wire. Plastic jalne ki smell = insulation damage.
🔌
Load Ek-Ek Karke Lagao
MCB trip ho — sab disconnect, phir ek ek load lagao. Jis load pe trip ho woh faulty hai.
Panel Wiring Tricks
📏
Wire Length Sahi Kato
Napke kato — terminal tak seedha route. Cable ties se bundle karo — neat dikhta hai.
🎨
Color Coding Follow Karo
Red/Brown = Phase, Black/Blue = Neutral, Green-Yellow = Earth. Ferrule (number tubes) lagao har wire pe.
🔢
Ferrule (Number Tag) Lagao
Har wire pe number tube lagao. Terminal block aur wire dono pe same number ho.
🔧
Terminal Tightening
Stranded wire ke end pe bootlace ferrule lagao phir connect karo. Loose = overheating.
Cable Pulling Tips
🥁
Drum Ko Khada Rakho
Flat mat rakho — cable twist ho jata hai. Hamesha flange pe khada rakho. Upar se nikalo.
🧴
Cable Pulling Lubricant
Long conduit mein cable pulling gel ya talcum powder use karo. Soap mat use karo.
📐
Bend Radius Ka Dhyan
Min bend radius = 8× cable OD (armoured). Tight bend se insulation crack hoti hai.
🔖
Cable Tag Immediately
Cable daal ke turant tag lagao — from, to, size, circuit number.

11kV transmission line. MSEB, TNEB jaise companies supply karti hain.

11kV ko 230V/415V mein convert karta hai. Pillar pe ya ground pe.

2-wire (L+N) ya 4-wire (R+Y+B+N). Underground ya overhead.
↓
4

Units (kWh) count karta hai. Smart meter digital reading deta hai.
↓

Poore ghar ki bijli band karo yahan se. Emergency mein sabse pehle yahi band karo!

Emergency Guide
🚨 Emergency mein: Main switch TURANT band karo!

Ghar ka main MCB ya fuse off karo. Jaldi karo!
2

3

Problem Diagnosis
MCB baar baar trip ho raha hai
+
Overload ya short circuit. Sab appliances band karo, MCB on karo, ek ek karke lagao — jis pe trip ho woh faulty hai.
Socket se spark aa raha hai
+
Loose connection ya worn out socket. Turant band karo. Electrician se replace karwao.
Lights flickering kar rahi hain
+
Loose wire, faulty bulb holder, ya voltage fluctuation. Pehle bulb change karke dekho.
Switch on karne pe shock lagta hai
+
Earth wire missing ya loose. Dangerous — electrician se check karwao turant.
Ghar mein burning smell
+
Wire overheating ya loose connection. Main switch band karo. Electrician ko call karo.

🏠 India Domestic Q&A
🏠 Domestic electrician interview ke liye common questions.
Ghar ki wiring mein kaun sa wire?
+
IS:694: Lights = 1.5mm² copper, Sockets = 2.5mm² copper, AC/Geyser = 4mm² copper, Earth = Green-Yellow. Hamesha ISI marked wire use karo.
MCB aur Fuse mein kya farq?
+
MCB: Reset ho sakta hai, fast, accurate, long life. Fuse: Replace karna padta hai, slow. MCB better hai.
RCCB kyon zaroori hai?
+
Earth leakage pe 30mA pe trip — insaan ki jaan bachata hai. MCB sirf overload/short circuit se bachata hai. Dono chahiye.
Earthing kyun zaroori hai?
+
Fault current ko safely earth mein bhejna, shock se bachana. IS:3043: residential earthing <5Ω honi chahiye.
Switch hamesha live wire pe kyun?
+
Safety ke liye. Neutral pe switch lage toh switch off hone pe bhi fixture pe live current rahega.
3-pin plug ka connection?
+
Top left = Neutral (Blue), Top right = Live (Red/Brown), Bottom big = Earth (Green-Yellow). Earth pin pehle enter hota hai.
AC ke liye dedicated circuit kyun?
+
AC high starting current leta hai. Shared circuit pe other loads pe voltage drop aata hai. Dedicated 20A MCB lagao.
Wire color code India mein?
+
IS:732: Red = R Phase/Live, Yellow = Y Phase, Blue = Neutral (new), Green-Yellow = Earth, Black = Old neutral.
Insulation resistance test kya hai?
+
Megger se 500V DC apply karke resistance check karna. Minimum 1 MΩ. New wiring commission se pehle mandatory.
PVC conduit aur GI conduit farq?
+
PVC: Cheap, light, domestic. GI: Heavy, strong, fire resistant, industrial/outdoor. Gulf mein GI zyada.
🏭 India Industrial Q&A
DOL aur Star-Delta mein farq?
+
DOL: Full voltage seedha. High starting current (6-8× FLC). 5HP tak. Star-Delta: Pehle star (57.7%) phir delta. Starting current 1/3 kam. 5HP+ ke liye standard.
Full Load Current kaise calculate?
+
3-Phase: FLC = (kW × 1000) ÷ (√3 × V × PF × η). Example: 15kW, 415V, PF=0.85, η=0.9 → FLC ≈ 27A.
VFD ka kya kaam hai?
+
Variable Frequency Drive — motor ki speed frequency badal ke control karta hai. 20-50% energy saving. Soft start. Pumps, fans pe use.
Power Factor kya hai?
+
PF = kW / kVA. Ideal = 1.0. Motors PF kam karte hain. Electricity board penalty lagata hai. Capacitor bank se improve hoti hai.
Important IS codes?
+
IS:732 (Wiring), IS:3043 (Earthing), IS:1554 (PVC cables), IS:8623 (DB panels), IS:2516 (Circuit breakers), IS:694 (House wiring), IS:12640 (RCCB).
MCC mein kya hota hai?
+
Motor Control Centre: Incomer MCCB, copper bus bar, individual motor feeders (MCCB + Contactor + Overload relay).
Cable tray fill factor?
+
IS standard: 40% maximum. Tray area ka 40% se zyada cables nahi. 60% air circulation ke liye.
Transformer KVA kaise select?
+
KVA = (Total kW × Demand Factor) ÷ PF × 1.25. Example: 100kW, PF=0.85, DF=0.8 → ~118 KVA → Select 125 KVA.
Earthing resistance values?
+
IS:3043: Residential <5Ω, Industrial <2Ω, Power Station <1Ω, Lightning arrester <10Ω.
Busbar current density?
+
Copper: 1.5 A/mm² safe. Aluminium: 1.0 A/mm² safe. Example: 100×10mm copper = 1500A safe current.

🏠 Gulf Domestic Q&A
DEWA kya hai?
+
Dubai Electricity & Water Authority. Dubai mein bijli aur paani supply. Electrical installations DEWA regulations ke hisab se honi chahiye. Approval mandatory.
Gulf mein wire color code?
+
IEC 60446: L1=Brown, L2=Black, L3=Grey, Neutral=Blue, Earth=Green-Yellow. India se alag — site pe pehle confirm karo.
Gulf mein open wiring kyun nahi?
+
Gulf regulations mein sab wiring conduit (PVC ya GI) mein honi chahiye. DEWA/ADDC inspectors check karte hain.
Gulf earthing system?
+
TN-S system standard. Earth resistance <1Ω. Copper clad steel rods. Quarterly inspection. Earth certificate authority ko submit karna hota hai.
Gulf mein socket types?
+
UK type (BS 1363) 13A 3-pin — especially UAE mein. Sab sockets earthed. Switched sockets standard practice.
ADDC, SEWA, KAHRAMAA kya hain?
+
ADDC = Abu Dhabi Distribution Company. SEWA = Sharjah Electricity. KAHRAMAA = Qatar General Electricity. Har authority ke apne regulations.
🏭 Gulf Industrial Q&A
🔥 Safety aur standards ka knowledge sabse important hai.
PTW kya hota hai?
+
Permit to Work. Electrical kaam shuru se pehle written permission leni hoti hai. Types: Hot Work, Cold Work, Confined Space, Electrical Work Permit. Bina PTW = immediate termination.
LOTO procedure step by step?
+
1) Notify workers. 2) Equipment shutdown. 3) Isolate energy. 4) Personal lock lagao. 5) Tag lagao. 6) Stored energy release. 7) Verify isolation. 8) Kaam karo. 9) Remove tools. 10) Remove lock. 11) Re-energize.
Hazardous Area classification?
+
IEC 60079: Zone 0 = Always explosive. Zone 1 = Normal operation mein explosive. Zone 2 = Abnormal conditions mein. ATEX/Ex rated equipment zaroor.
Aramco electrical standards?
+
SAES = Saudi Aramco Engineering Standards. SAES-P-100, P-104, P-111. Very strict QC/QA. Method Statement aur Risk Assessment mandatory.
ELV systems ka scope?
+
ELV = Extra Low Voltage (<50V AC). Gulf scope: CCTV, Access Control, Fire Alarm, PA System, BMS, Structured Cabling (LAN). ELV knowledge extra salary deta hai.
India aur Gulf mein sabse bada farq?
+
1) PTW/LOTO mandatory. 2) PPE hamesha. 3) Open wiring nahi. 4) IEC standards. 5) English communication. 6) Documentation zaroori. 7) Strict inspection.
A-Z Electrical Materials
🔤 Har electrical material ki basic jankari.
A
Armoured Cable (SWA/AWA)
Steel/Aluminium wire armoured cable. Underground aur outdoor use. Mechanical protection. XLPE ya PVC insulation.
B
Busbar
Copper ya Aluminium flat bar — panel mein power distribute karta hai. 25×3mm se 160×10mm tak.
C
Conduit (PVC / GI)
Wire protect karne ke liye pipe. PVC: Domestic. GI: Industrial, outdoor. 20mm se 63mm sizes.
D
Distribution Board (DB)
MCB aur RCCB ka panel. IS:8623 standard. 4-way se 40-way tak.
E
Earth Rod / ELCB
Earth Rod: Copper clad steel, 16mm dia, 3m long. ELCB = Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker (old name for RCCB).
F
Fuse (HRC / Rewirable)
HRC = High Rupturing Capacity. Fast, industrial. Rewirable/Kitkat: Old type. IS:9224.
G
Gland (Cable Gland)
Cable ko enclosure mein seal karna. M-type: M16-M75. PG type: PG7-PG36. SWA ke liye double compression.
H
Heat Shrink Tube
Wire joints insulate karna. Heat lagane pe shrink — tight seal. Electrical tape se better.
I
Isolator Switch
Circuit ko completely isolate karna. Hamesha no-load pe operate karo.
J
Junction Box
Wire joints cover karna. IP44 = weather resistant, IP65 = dust+water proof.
K
KWh Meter (Energy Meter)
Bijli consumption measure karta hai. Smart meter digital reading. CT operated meter bade loads ke liye.
L
Lug (Cable Lug)
Cable end pe terminal ke liye. Copper compression lug. Crimping tool se lagao — solder nahi.
M
MCB / MCCB / Meter
MCB: 1A-125A domestic. MCCB: 100A-1600A industrial, adjustable trip.
N
Neutral Link / Neutral Bar
Panel mein sab neutral wires yahan connect. Solid copper bar.
O
Overload Relay (OLR)
Motor ko overloading se bachata hai. FLC ka 1.05-1.15× pe set karo.
P
PVC Cable / Panel Board
PVC = Polyvinyl Chloride insulation. IS:694 standard. Panel Board = DB/MDB/SMDB.
Q
Quick Connect Terminal (Wago)
Push-in terminal — tool ke bina wire connect. Spring clamp technology.
R
RCCB / Relay / Rectifier
RCCB: Earth leakage protection. Relay: Control circuit switching. Rectifier: AC to DC convert.
S
Switch / Socket / SWA Cable
Switch: IS:3854. Socket: IS:1293. SWA = Steel Wire Armoured. IS:1554.
T
Transformer / Terminal Block
Transformer: Voltage step up/down. IS:4722. Terminal Block: DIN rail mount wire connections.
U
UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply)
Power failure pe backup. Online = zero switchover. Offline = 10-25ms. VA = Load ÷ PF.
V
VFD (Variable Frequency Drive)
Motor speed control. 0-50Hz adjustable. 20-50% energy saving. Pumps, fans, compressors.
W
Wire / Wago / Wire Loom
Wire: IS:694 domestic, IS:1554 industrial. Wago = Push-in connector. Wire Loom = Spiral wrap.
X
XLPE Cable
Cross-Linked Polyethylene insulation. IS:7098. 90°C temp rating (vs 70°C PVC). Industrial power cables.
Y
Y-Connection (Star)
3-Phase motor winding connection. 415V system mein phase voltage = 240V.
Z
Zener Diode / Zigzag Transformer
Zener: Control circuit voltage regulation. Zigzag Transformer: Earthing transformer.
